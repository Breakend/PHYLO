Description of the scoring system

The scores of registered users is based on the number of puzzles that improve the original DNA sequence alignment.

We re-inserted all puzzle solutions at their original position in the 44-species multiple sequence alignment. Players have been credited of 1 point for every solution that increased the score of the complete genome alignment. Of course, duplicates have been removed. We report various rankings:

General ranking: All puzzles considered. The players are sorted according to their total score.

First contribution ranking: Only the first player who found the solution that have been inserted received 1 point for the puzzle. Other received 0 points.

Disease category ranking: Only the puzzles whose sequences correspond to one gene related to a disease belonging to the specified category are considered. The list of categories and related diseases is described in the disease classification section.
